Find the lineage of a result

Submitted by admin on Mon, 04/30/2018 - 22:41
Contributor: 
Anila Butt
Goal: 
The use case provides the ability to repeat the workflow known business processes on original data and reproduce the results
Summary: 
The user wants to be able to view the provenance of a result, including the workflow which generated this result. The provenance of a result should also explain when the result was generated, who is responsible for it, what dataset(s) have been used to generate it and what business rules are consulted to conclude the result.
